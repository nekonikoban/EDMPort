Short Introduction

You can either drag&drop your audio/mp3 file into marked area ,or just click on it and find your audio file manually. You can also play tracks from Deezer API & local database which includes about 60 samples.


Player track has regular play/repeat/volume/timeline controls including animation styles and cinematic effect
When audio is loaded simply click on the song name to copy it to the clipboard.

If you are want to listen to my personal collection next page will bring you to the gallery where you can play tracks from a local database.
